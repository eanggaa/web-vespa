ADVENTURES HAPPEN ON TWO WHEELS

Passionate, intrepid travellers need solid reliability to get them places, which is why the Vespa GTS is the perfect travel companion. Its urban spirit, seen in the large,technologically-endowed frame that offers reassurance and protection, is matched by a love of long-distance travel.

Engine technology

The 300 High Performance Engine simultaneously delivers fantastic ridability and reduced fuel consumption and noise. The 125 i-get uses Start & Stop RISS (Regulator Inverter Start & Stop System), meaning quiet start-up, increased lightness and greater reliability than ever.

Bike Finder

The Vespa GTS range includes a remote control that opens up the seat from a distance, a USB port in the rear shield compartment and the Bike Finder function that makes it easy to pick out your parked bike in a crowd.

Full LED

Both the headlight and rear lights make use of full LED for improved visibility in all riding conditions.
